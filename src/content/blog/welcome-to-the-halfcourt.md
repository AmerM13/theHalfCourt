Another NBA blog. I know. Here's the pitch anyway.

The HalfCourt is for the games between the games — the rotation tweak that flipped a series, the sets a team runs out of a timeout, the box-score line that says more than the highlight did. Less takes-for-the-timeline, more actually-watched-the-tape.

## What to expect

- **Film breakdowns** — a possession, a coverage, a counter, explained.
- **Box-score deep dives** — the numbers behind a win nobody's talking about.
- **Trade and signing reactions** — fast, but not just noise.
- **The occasional bad take** — defended with full confidence.

Posts run through the season. The best of it goes out in the newsletter — sign up on the [home page](/).

Tip-off's coming. See you on the court.
